World Cup Prediction Tool

VS

Instructions
To use this program, you will need to select two teams. This can be done using the drop down lists above. The first team you select, the one on the left, will be the "home" team. The second team you select, the one on the right, will be the "away" team. Once you've selected your teams, go ahead and press the submit button! Note that since a team cannot play against itself, you should not attempt to enter the same team in both lists.

Explanation
This program works by taking the results from previous world cup tournaments, and using that information to train itself. Every time the "submit" button is pressed, the program will look at how both teams have historically performed, and try to figure out which would win today. However, the training occurs slightly differently every time the program is run. This means that the results might vary from one use to another. We reccomend that you pay close attention to the accuracy displayed alongside the result!
